## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 🧪 Demo Examination Platform
Solves the challenge of manual, static exam creation by offering an AI-powered examination platform with three distinct modes — Practice, Timed, and Survival. It leverages OpenRouter to dynamically generate multiple-choice questions on any topic, persists them in Supabase, and tracks user stats and achievements locally — making exam preparation smarter, more adaptive, and fully automated.  
🔗 [Repo](https://github.com/Redietkebede/demo-examination-platform) · 🌐 [Live Demo](https://demo-examination-platform.vercel.app)

---

### 🏋️ Gym Booking System
Eliminates the friction of manual gym scheduling by providing a full-stack booking and management platform for premium training studios. Members can browse services and submit bookings through a clean public interface, while admins get a secure, authenticated dashboard to manage bookings, services, and statuses in real time — all backed by Prisma ORM and PostgreSQL.  
🔗 [Repo](https://github.com/Redietkebede/gym_booking_system) · 🌐 [Live Demo](https://gym-booking-system-one.vercel.app)

---

### 🤖 AI Learning Path Generator Platform
Addresses the problem of unstructured self-learning by generating personalized, step-by-step learning roadmaps tailored to a user's goals and skill level. Powered by OpenRouter LLMs with Supabase persistence, it also features lead capture, event tracking, SEO-optimized blog routes, and a fully animated SaaS-style landing page — turning learning direction from guesswork into a guided journey.  
🔗 [Repo](https://github.com/Redietkebede/AI-Learning-Path-Generator-Platform) · 🌐 [Live Demo](https://ai-learning-path-generator-platform.vercel.app)

---

### 📊 Financial & Economic Intelligence Dashboard
Tackles the complexity of interpreting raw economic and market data by building a full end-to-end intelligence platform. It ingests macro indicators from FRED and market data from Alpha Vantage via a Python ETL pipeline, computes derived signals like Macro Risk Index and Currency Strength Rankings, and delivers them through a responsive Next.js dashboard — turning fragmented public datasets into clear, decision-ready financial insights.  
🔗 [Repo](https://github.com/Redietkebede/Financial-Economic-Intelligence-Dashboard) · 🌐 [Live Demo](https://financial-economic-intelligence-monitoring.vercel.app/)
